Control PC & Stream from Any Device — Fully Local.

ZenDeck is a powerful, self-hosted virtual Stream Deck. Control OBS, manage media, trigger macros, and monitor PC stats. No cloud. No accounts.

Download for Windows Support the Project

More Than Just a Stream Deck

Everything you need to control your workspace, built with privacy in mind.

🌐

Self-Hosted Web Interface

ZenDeck runs a local web server on your PC, allowing access from any device on your network via browser.

⌨️

Macros & Shortcuts

Execute complex keyboard strokes, create stateful toggle keys, and instantly open your favorite websites.

🎵

Media Control & Widgets

Take full control of your audio. Play, pause, skip tracks, and display beautiful, real-time media widgets.

📊

Real-Time PC Stats

Keep an eye on your hardware. Monitor system performance, CPU, and RAM usage directly on your deck.

🎥

Native OBS Integration

Direct connection to OBS Studio with seamless scene switching, source toggles, and live status feedback.

🔒

No Cloud. No Accounts.

Everything runs completely locally on your machine. No sign-ups, no subscriptions, no external servers.
